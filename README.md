# Cosmetics Company KPI Analysis
## About the Project
I built an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze sales performance, profitability, and customer data for an e-commerce retail store (365 Cosmetics). The main goal was to take raw transaction data and turn it into clear, scannable insights that show exactly where the business is making money and where it's losing it.

Instead of sticking with standard Power BI templates, I designed a custom user interface from scratch using a cohesive color palette (Dusty Rose and Charcoal) with soft-rounded containers to make the data easy on the eyes.

## Dashboard Structure

### 1. Overview Page
* **Core Metrics:** Displays high-level KPIs including 51K total customers, $1.1M total net profit, $6.52M in sales, and an overall profit margin of 16%.
* **Product Performance:** Breaks down sales and profit by main categories like Body Care, Make Up, Face Care, and Hair Care to see which lines drive the most revenue.
* **Monthly Sales Trend:** Tracks monthly performance from January to December to point out seasonal peaks and drops throughout the year.
* **Profit Margin vs. Discount:** A trend chart analyzing how discounting affects the bottom line, showing exactly where promotions start hurting profitability.
* **Top Products:** A clean matrix view of individual items to monitor high-volume SKU performance.

### 2. Regional Analysis
* **Sales Map:** Shows geographic distribution across regions like Western Europe, Central America, Oceania, and the US.
* **Market Trends (2020–2023):** Tracks how sales grew year-over-year across different global markets (Africa, APAC, Europe, LATAM, USCA).
* **Country Highlights:** A data table isolating top-margin countries (like Gambia, Guyana, and Slovenia) to find the most profitable locations.

### 3. Customer Demographics
* **Employment Status Breakdown:** Splits the 51K customer base into three segments: Consumer (51.7%), Corporate (30%), and Self-Employed (18%).
* **High-Value Customers:** A table tracking specific customer IDs, their locations, and their profit margins to see who the top buyers are.
* **Yearly Growth:** A bar chart showing total customers served each year from 2020 to 2023 to monitor growth.
